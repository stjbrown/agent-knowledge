# Operations

The three workflows that keep an [LLM Wiki](../concepts/llm_wiki.md) alive. These are the behaviors
the portable OKF skills will implement; the pages here capture the *what* and *why* independent of
any specific skill runtime.

* [Ingest](./ingest.md) - read a source, extract signal, and integrate it across the bundle.
* [Query](./query.md) - navigate, synthesize an answer with citations, and file valuable answers back.
* [Lint](./lint.md) - health-check the bundle for contradictions, stale claims, orphans, and gaps.
